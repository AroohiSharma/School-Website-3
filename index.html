Campus life

APOSTOLIC CARMEL HIGH SCHOOL

Welcome to our home on the internet. Although we have invested in technology in our classrooms, we have been late being on the Internet. Over the last 26 years, our school has provided girls in Bandra West a value based education. We now have over 2000 students and have started investing in technology to aid the teachers with interactive content. We will continue to develop and enrich our student's education and life.

SECONDARY CLASSES

Apostolic Carmel High School is registered as an English-Medium High School which prepares its students for the S.S.C. Examination. The second and third languages taught in the school are compulsory Marathi and Hindi from Standards V – X.There is a paradigm shift in the curriculum as well as in the teaching for our students as they now have to learn specific subjects as well as gear up for exams.Teachers use various techniques to inculcate the discipline of learning in them. They encourage students to perform role-plays, make projects, grasp the practical knowledge of the subject and promote creativity through prose and poetry pieces created by the students.

Our Values
Our Values

Through a broad range of experiences, our students become confident, optimistic, compassionate and ready to find their place in the world. Our aim at ACICSE is to provide an environment for each and every child where they feel safe, secure, happy and motivated to learn. In this way each child progresses academically as well as developing their own unique talents. The values at the heart of our school are – Creativity, Pride, Responsibility, Confidence, Perseverance, Teamwork, Aspiration & Enjoyment. Through these values we strive to ensure that our child's first steps on the ladder of education are happy and rewarding.

Center of excellence for kids
Center of excellence for kids

A variety of activities like Speech and Drama sessions, Skating, Karate and Yoga, Field trips, inter class competitions, etc. are included in our curriculum to provide opportunities to our students to develop a wholesome personality. We, at ACICSE, pride ourselves on having a staff team committed to providing the best for every child, preparing them to face their future.

Sports & games
Sports & games

Students are segregated into four houses namely Red House, Yellow House, Blue House and Green House. Intra-mural games and sports are conducted to promote healthy competitions among the four houses where the adrenaline is gushed up as they take part in all events energetically.
